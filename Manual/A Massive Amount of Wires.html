On the Subject of A Massive Amount of Wires

What? The title says it all. Did you expect a more creative name?

This module contains 150-200 wires in a variety of colors.

To solve the module simply cut all wires of the first color in the list below. Then cut all wires of the second color and so on.

Cutting a wire out of order will result in a strike.

Color Cut Order

  • White
  • Grey
  • Black
  • Red
  • Orange
  • Yellow
  • Lime
  • Green
  • Jade
  • Cyan
  • Azure
  • Blue
  • Violet
  • Magenta
  • Rose
